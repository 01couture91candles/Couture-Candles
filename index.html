Couture Candles began with a passion for creating candles that are not just sources of light, but pieces of art that elevate your space. Each candle is meticulously hand-poured using the finest natural ingredients.

Frequently Asked Questions

What wax do you use in your candles?

We use 100% natural soy wax which is eco-friendly, burns cleaner and longer than paraffin wax, and provides excellent fragrance throw.

How long do your candles typically burn?

Our standard candles (200g) burn for approximately 40-50 hours. Larger candles can burn for 80-100 hours depending on size and burning conditions.

Do you offer international shipping?

Currently we ship within India only. For international inquiries, please contact us via WhatsApp or Instagram for special arrangements.

Can I customize candles for special events?

Yes! We offer customization for weddings, corporate events, and other special occasions. Contact us with your requirements via WhatsApp at +91 8595618629.

Shipping Policy

We ship across India within 3-5 business days after order confirmation. Shipping is free for orders above ₹999. For orders below ₹999, a flat shipping fee of ₹99 applies.

We partner with reliable courier services to ensure your candles reach you safely. All candles are carefully packaged to prevent damage during transit.

For urgent deliveries, please contact us via WhatsApp at +91 85956 18629 for special arrangements.

Returns & Exchanges

We want you to be completely satisfied with your purchase. If you're not happy with your candle, you may return it within 7 days of delivery for a full refund or exchange.

To be eligible for a return, your item must be unused and in the same condition that you received it. It must also be in the original packaging.

To initiate a return, please contact us via WhatsApp or Instagram with your order details and reason for return.

Track Your Order

Once your order has been shipped, you will receive a confirmation email with your tracking number and a link to track your package.

If you haven't received your tracking information within 3 business days or need help tracking your order, please contact us via WhatsApp at +91 85956 18629.

Wholesale Inquiries

We offer wholesale pricing for retailers, event planners, and businesses looking to stock our premium candles.

Wholesale orders require a minimum purchase of ₹15,000. Discounts increase with order volume.

For wholesale inquiries, please contact us via WhatsApp at +91 85956 18629 or email at wholesale@couturecandles.com with your business details and requirements.
